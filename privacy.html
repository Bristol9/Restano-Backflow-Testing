Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Restano Backflow Testing ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and property address when you submit an appointment request or contact us.
  • Service Information: Details about your backflow prevention device, property type, and water authority requirements.
  • Usage Information: Basic analytics about how visitors use our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and perform backflow testing services
  • Communicate with you about appointments, results, and compliance
  • Submit test results to water authorities when applicable
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Water authorities or compliance databases as required for backflow testing certification
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., payment processors, when activated)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements
