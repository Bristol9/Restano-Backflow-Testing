Annual Notice

Received a Backflow Testing Notice?

If you have received an annual backflow testing notice from your water authority, Restano Backflow Testing can test the device, prepare the required documentation, and submit the results electronically when applicable.

Backflow prevention devices help protect the public water supply. Water authorities require annual testing by a certified tester to ensure the device is operating correctly. Failure to complete required testing may affect compliance and can potentially result in a water service interruption.
